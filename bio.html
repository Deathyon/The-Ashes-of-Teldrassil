Hi, I'm Adam and I'm a developer in Colorado with a passion for design and creation. Since I was a kid I have always loved video games, drawing, and designing. When I was younger I would create and script maps for Starcraft, draw characters and comics, and create backgrounds for my desktop or myspace page.



Now I have taken all of those same skills to the next level as a professional who has experience: designing and programming PC games in Unreal Engine 4 or Unity3D, designing UI/UX and writing C++ code for professional software, and creating web pages and book/movie covers.



I recently moved from Orlando, Florida to Colorado with my girlfriend, Erika and we are both loving the Colorado climate. I am also working on a mobile game in Unity that brings back the nostalgia of digital pets from the '90s and playing a lot of Destiny 2 on PS4!
